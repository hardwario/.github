## Why integrators choose HARDWARIO

- **No gatekeeping.** Datasheets, APIs, and firmware SDKs are public — everything you need to evaluate, integrate, and ship is in these repositories and [docs.hardwario.com](https://docs.hardwario.com/). Fork it, customize it, ship it.
- **Speed to revenue.** A typical white-label deployment goes from first call to live data in **2–4 weeks**, backed by a catalog of 15+ ready-made applications.
- **Field-proven hardware.** Devices run in 30+ countries, rated **−20 to +60 °C** (field-proven to −40 °C), IP67, CE/FCC certified, with **3+ years** on primary lithium cells.
- **European supply chain.** Developed and manufactured in the EU, with 10+ year product longevity — no surprise end-of-life in the middle of your rollout.

## Start here

1. **Read the docs** — [docs.hardwario.com](https://docs.hardwario.com/) covers hardware, firmware, and connectivity end to end.
2. **Get a DevKit** — the [CHESTER DevKit](https://www.hardwario.com/products/chester/) is the fastest way to a proof of concept on your desk.
3. **Clone the SDK** — [`chester-sdk`](https://github.com/hardwario/chester-sdk) (Zephyr RTOS / nRF Connect SDK, C) builds on Windows, macOS, and Linux with VS Code.
4. **Talk to us** — [book an exploration call](https://www.hardwario.com/book/) or [contact us](https://www.hardwario.com/contact/).

## Open source highlights

1. [`chester-sdk`](https://github.com/hardwario/chester-sdk) — firmware SDK for the CHESTER platform, built on Zephyr RTOS and the nRF Connect SDK
2. [`tower-firmware`](https://github.com/hardwario/tower-firmware) — Rust/Embassy firmware SDK for the open TOWER prototyping ecosystem
3. [`tower-cli`](https://github.com/hardwario/tower-cli) — host-side CLI/TUI that streams the framed serial console and flashes devices over UART
4. [`py-hardwario`](https://github.com/hardwario/py-hardwario) — command-line tools for device console, flashing, and HARDWARIO Cloud
5. [`lora-modem`](https://github.com/hardwario/lora-modem) — open LoRaWAN modem firmware for the Murata Type ABZ module
